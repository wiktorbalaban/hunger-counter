Privacy Policy

Hunger Counter  ·  Last updated: May 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Hunger Counter mobile application ("the App") handles your information. The App is developed by Wiktor Bałaban ("I", "me").

1. Data Collected

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Date and time of hunger episodes
  • Duration of each episode (in minutes)
  • Intensity level (low, medium, or high)
  • Whether concentration problems were noted during the episode

The App also stores technical state data locally: the current app icon variant and the date the daily icon reset last ran. This data is not personal and is used only to manage the app icon appearance.

2. Purpose of Processing

The data listed above is used solely to display your personal hunger history within the App (Today tab and Report tab). It is never used for any other purpose.

3. Storage and Retention

All data is stored exclusively on your device using local device storage. No data leaves your device. There is no account system, no server, and no cloud sync.

Data is retained indefinitely until you uninstall the App or clear the App's data manually via your device's App Settings.

4. Third-Party Sharing

No data is shared with, sold to, or transmitted to any third party. The App contains no advertising SDK, no analytics SDK, and no crash reporting service.

5. Background Processing

On Android, the App registers a background task that runs approximately once per hour. This task checks whether the current date has changed in order to reset the app icon. It accesses only the locally stored hunger entries described above and does not transmit any data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all data stored by the App at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the App, or
  • Going to your device Settings → Apps → Hunger Counter → Clear Data

Because all data is stored locally on your device and I have no access to it, I am unable to retrieve or delete it on your behalf.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, I will update the "Last updated" date above. The current version is always available at this URL.
